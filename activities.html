The Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is ajacent to a state park. Go it alone or join one of our guided hikes.

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use.

Bird Watching

While anytime is as good for bird watching at Pacific Trails, we offer guided bird watching trips at sunrise several times a week.
